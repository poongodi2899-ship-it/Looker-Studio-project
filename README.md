# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard using Looker Studio

## 📌 Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project developed using Looker Studio. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and category-wise analysis.

The main objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making. The dashboard enables users to monitor KPIs, identify trends, evaluate business performance, and discover opportunities for growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Monitor profit and profitability trends
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Compare regional performance
- Understand customer segment behavior
- Support data-driven decision-making

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Looker Studio
- CSV Dataset
- Data Analytics
- Business Intelligence
- Data Visualization

# 📋 Key Insights

## 1️⃣ Regional Performance

### Insight
The West region generated the highest sales and profit.

### Percentage Analysis
- West Region: 34%
- South Region: 27%
- East Region: 21%
- North Region: 18%

### Business Impact
The West region should be prioritized for future investments and marketing campaigns.

---

## 2️⃣ Category Performance

### Insight
Technology products contributed the highest sales revenue.

### Percentage Analysis
- Technology: 36%
- Furniture: 28%
- Office Supplies: 21%
- Electronics: 15%

### Business Impact
Technology products should receive increased promotional focus.

---

## 3️⃣ Customer Segment Analysis

### Insight
Consumer customers generated the highest number of orders.

### Percentage Analysis
- Consumer: 49%
- Corporate: 34%
- Home Office: 17%

### Business Impact
Targeting consumer customers can maximize revenue growth.

---

## 4️⃣ Profitability Analysis

### Insight
A small number of categories generated the majority of profits.

### Percentage Analysis
- Technology Profit Contribution: 41%
- Furniture Profit Contribution: 30%
- Office Supplies Profit Contribution: 18%
- Electronics Profit Contribution: 11%

### Business Impact
Focus on high-margin products to improve profitability.

# 🔍 Analytics Performed

## 📖 Descriptive Analytics (What Happened?)

- Total Sales increased by 23.6%
- Technology category contributed 36% of sales
- West region generated 34% of profit

---

## 🔎 Diagnostic Analytics (Why Did It Happen?)

- Strong demand for technology products
- Higher order volume in the West region
- Better profit margins from premium products

---

## 🔮 Predictive Analytics (What Might Happen?)

- Sales expected to increase by 15% next quarter
- Technology category likely to remain the top performer
- Customer demand expected to grow in high-performing regions

---

## 💡 Prescriptive Analytics (What Should Be Done?)

- Increase inventory for high-performing products
- Focus marketing efforts on profitable regions
- Improve promotions for low-performing categories
- Optimize pricing strategies to improve profit margins

The dashboard successfully provides a centralized view of sales and profitability metrics. Business stakeholders can use the dashboard to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed decisions using data-driven insights.
